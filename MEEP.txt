Primary Research Question: Does decomposing verification uncertainty into failure-mode-specific signals and learning a routing policy over that decomposition improve risk-coverage performance, relative to routing on a single scalar confidence score?

2. Experimental Philosophy
1. 
Honest and Rigorous Reporting: All metrics are calculated blindly without post-hoc cherry-picking.
2. 
Strict Separation of Tiers: Component validation is explicitly separated from hypothesis verification.
3. 
Falsifiability as Priority: Every experiment is framed such that the null hypothesis can be established deterministically.

7.1 Human Evaluation Protocol (H4 Assessment)
To test H4, triage escalation decisions must be correlated against human judgment.
• 
Sampling Strategy: 50 claims selected via stratified sampling from the FEVER dev set (ensuring equal representation of length and entity density).
• 
Annotation Instructions: Annotators evaluate if the claim is "Definitively Answerable" or "Genuinely Ambiguous/Hard" based exclusively on the provided context.
• 
Annotators: Minimum of two independent annotators (if solo researcher, use a peer or perform self-annotation with a 14-day blinded delay).
• 
Agreement Metric: Cohen's Kappa is calculated between human consensus and system 'ESCALATE' flags. A Kappa > 0.41 (Moderate) constitutes a successful correlation pass.

9. Calibration Evaluation
Reliability Diagrams plot empirical accuracy against predicted confidence across 10 evenly spaced bins. ECE (Expected Calibration Error) and Brier scores are tracked. This is a critical validation gate before computing epistemic uncertainty.
10. Statistical Methodology
• 
Deterministic Seeding: Fixed random seeds [42, 137, 732].
• 
Significance Testing: Two-tailed paired t-test over bootstrap distributions, alpha threshold of p < 0.05.
11. Error Analysis Protocol
1. 
Retrieval Insufficiency Type: Gold evidence missing from Top-K.
2. 
Evidence Conflict Type: Overlapping index records return opposing truth.
3. 
Epistemic Failure Type: High-confidence output despite extreme variance.
4. 
Routing Error Type: Incorrect downstream assignment.
